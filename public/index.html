Reflection/Connection

Reflection/Connection is a participatory community art project, an exercise in collective memory making for this time in isolation. You are invited to contribute a short video self-portrait of 10-20s, which will be filtered with through piece of generative code art, schemaVideoVariabile, and added to the Reflection/Connection collection. Initially the collection will be displayed on a dedicated Instagram account and on this website, ideally the filtered video portraits will be presented in a physical installation some time in the future when that becomes possible again.

How to Shoot the Self-Portrait
This will take 5 minutes. There is no need to rush.
  • Get comfortable.
  • Get your phone out, open the camera.
  • Line up the shot, get ready to record.
  • Close your eyes.
  • Really take this moment to get settled.
  • Notice the muscles in your face. Release any tension.
  • Breathe
  • Keep you eyes closed.
  • Start recording.
  • When you are ready, open your eyes.
  • Look directly into the camera, not the screen.
  • Record for as long as you like

Upload Instructions

Please use WeTransfer to send your video to: reflectionconnectionproject@gmail.com

Your contribution will be anonymous by default.

If you would like to be tagged on instagram, please include your instagram handle.

The videos will become quite distorted, you will be hard to recognise, but not impossible.
If you would prefer to be unrecognisable, please write "diguise me!" in your message.
